Grilled Chicken Recipe

A high-protein grilled chicken recipe thatâ€™s perfect for a healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lemon, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the mixture over the chicken breasts.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for 6-7 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  5. Add lemon slices to the grill during the last 2 minutes of cooking.
  6. Remove from the grill and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
