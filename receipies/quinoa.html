Quinoa Salad Recipe

A refreshing and nutritious quinoa salad perfect for a healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water.
  2. Cook the quinoa in 2 cups of water for 15 minutes or until tender.
  3. Let the quinoa cool completely.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cucumber, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  6. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  7. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
